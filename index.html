Read the full story

Dub & Troll is the husband and wife songwriting duo turning everyday moments, cross country stories, and late night inspiration into quirky, feel good pop. As parents of four, they are no strangers to chaos, creativity, or the magic that happens when you mix the two, so their music leans bold, playful, and wonderfully unordinary. Blending crisp modern production with a spark of experimental imagination, Dub & Troll craft songs that feel both fresh and familiar, like classic melodies filtered through a new lens.

Their debut project, Christ Across America, is a nationwide Christmas adventure. Fifty states, fifty original holiday songs, each one capturing the spirit, traditions, and personality of its home. From snowy mountain towns to warm coastal nights, the album travels everywhere, carrying the joy, humor, and heart that define the band sound.

With a style that bounces between soulful storytelling and tongue in cheek fun, Dub & Troll bring a creative chemistry only two lifelong partners can share. Their music videos, shot and produced entirely by the pair, blend cinematic energy with everyday charm, offering fans a window into the world they are building together.

Dub & Troll make music for people who love to smile, dance, think, and feel everything in between. This is a band built on curiosity, wonder, and the belief that creativity, no matter where it starts, can bring people together.
